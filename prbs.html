ABOUT OUR TRANSMISSION:
Welcome to the PRBS television service portal! Because we cannot afford constant content production, our state-of-the-art broadcast operates on a strict daily loop. We pre-record a comprehensive 6-hour block filled with 15 and 30-minute entertainment, news, and cultural programming, and then loop it 4 times to cover the full 24-hour cycle. Grab some apple juice, sit back in front of the wall projector, and enjoy the scheduled programming.

Note: We definitely didn't steal this concept from Blippians. If we actually did, we are incredibly sorry, cause your Blippo+ system is actually well made!
